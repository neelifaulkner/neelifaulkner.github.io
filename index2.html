Who I Am Off The Record

I'm the founder of Murder Creek Media, where I lead a fully volunteer team of over 15 dedicated researchers, writers, and investigators.

My investigative and storytelling work has produced new evidence in two murder convictions and surfaced new leads in an unsolved cold case, giving families long-overdue hope for answers.

I'm a proud member of the National Society of Leadership and Success (NSLS) and Omega Nu Lambda, and in 2025, I was nationally recognized by the Alliance for Women in Media for my investigative work on Skeptical: An Investigative Podcast — a rare honor for someone in independent media.

Outside of my professional work, I dedicate my time to nonprofit projects supporting children with special needs and continue to advocate for truth, transparency, and justice through digital storytelling.
